Basics of Web Development

Web development is the art and science of creating websites and web applications that are functional, visually appealing, and user-friendly. It brings together design, programming, and problem-solving to deliver seamless digital experiences.

Key Principles of Web Development

  • User-Centric Design: Focus on making websites easy and enjoyable to use.
  • Responsive Layout: Ensure websites work perfectly on mobile, tablet, and desktop screens.
  • Performance: Optimize for fast loading and smooth interactions.
  • Content Quality: Provide meaningful, relevant, and engaging content for visitors.
